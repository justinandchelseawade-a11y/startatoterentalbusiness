Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2, 2026

We want startatoterentalbusiness.com to be usable by as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technology. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

What we have done

  • Used clear, semantic page structure and headings
  • Made the site keyboard-navigable with visible focus indicators
  • Provided text alternatives for meaningful images
  • Aimed for sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Included a "skip to content" link
  • Built the layout to be responsive and to work when text is enlarged

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is a continuing effort, and some parts of the site may not yet be perfect. We review and improve as we go.

Need help or found a problem?

If you have trouble accessing any part of this site, or you have a suggestion, please email justin@cowetatoterentals.com. Tell us the page and the issue, and we will do our best to help and to fix it.
